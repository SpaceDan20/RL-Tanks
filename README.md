## RL-Tanks

RL-Tanks is a RL project focused on building ML agents that learn to drive tanks in a battlefield and destroy enemy tanks. The concept uses Unity's ML-Agents and its self-play functionality to create a compelling 1 vs. 1 environment. The goal is to create the most capable agents possible and research the strategies that come out of this combative RL pipeline.

# Roadmap

- Train tanks to 1 v 1 competitively for a capture point
- Introduce 2 v 2
- Introduce a more complex map
- Add capture zones/objectives
- Introduce 3 v 3
- Experiment with unique gamemodes
- Experiment with 5 v 5 +
